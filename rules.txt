<h1>Euro 2020 Predictions Contest</h1><br>

<br>Correctly predict the result (win/draw/loss) of each of the 51 matches in Euro 2020 (which is actually in 2021).
<br>Predictions must be made before the scheduled kickoff of each individual match.
<br>A correct prediction is worth ...

<ul><li> 1 point for the group games (except the opening game).</li> 
<li> 2 points for the opening game, and the round of 16 games.</li>
<li> 3 points for the quarter finals.</li> 
<li> 4 points for the semi finals.</li>
<li> 10 points for the finale.</li></ul>
<br>
<br>The result of knock-out games is determined after extra-time or penalties where applicable.
<br>Winner is the person with the most points.
